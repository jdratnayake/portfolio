Electromagnetic Insights Acquisition Through a Forensics-as-a-Service Platform

Published in: Feb 5, 2025

  • This research introduces EMvidence, a platform designed to assist forensic investigators in analyzing Internet of Things (IoT) devices more efficiently using Electromagnetic Side-Channel Analysis (EM-SCA). Traditional EM-SCA methods require deep technical expertise, making investigations complex. EMvidence simplifies this process with two key tools: a data acquisition application for collecting Electromagnetic (EM) data from IoT devices and a cloud-based web application for analyzing the data.
