EMvidence
  • EMvidence is a software tool designed to extract forensic insights from electromagnetic (EM) signals emitted by IoT devices. It enables users to capture the EM signals emitted by the device being tested using Software-defined Radio (SDR) hardware named HackRF One. Users can preprocess and analyze these signals using EMvidence's various modules to gain an understanding of the tested IoT devices. Additionally, users have the flexibility to develop their own third-party modules tailored to their specific needs, promoting the open-source development of preprocessing and analysis plugins that enhance the overall capability of the software tool. EMvidence provides an alternative forensic solution for IoT devices that was found in an investigation instead of classical and invasive digital forensic techniques.
  • Contributors: Contributor 3
ARTTIC
  • ARTTIC is a community platform that develops and promotes NFT (Non-Fungible Token) creators, allowing them to market their work. It provides a global network between NFT creators and followers. Creators can verify their status by connecting their crypto wallet to the system, which is connected to OpenSea. The platform provides information on newly generated NFTs, their background stories, upcoming NFTs, and previous works of the creator. Creators can publish all the latest information on their profiles and receive feedback from followers. The platform is for existing NFT creators and followers, as well as anyone interested in joining the industry in the future.

ALec
  • ALec is a mobile and web application that addresses the challenges of low student engagement and interaction in the classroom. It allows lecturers to give small quizzes during lectures, which students can answer via their smartphones, giving the lecturer a real-time understanding of students' knowledge and understanding of the lecture. Students can also ask questions anonymously through the app, increasing participation and interaction in class. The app provides a platform for self-evaluation through a leaderboard and review system, helping students to pay closer attention to course content. ALec strengthens the interaction between lecturers and students, increasing efficiency and popularity for both parties, and ultimately improving the overall learning experience.
